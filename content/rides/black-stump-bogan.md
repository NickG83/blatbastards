**Day 1;** Muster point in Gunning at the Old Hume Cafe, before heading through the backroads via Crookwell to Bathurst and a lap of Mt Panorama. Late lunch in Hill End. From Hill End, heading for the overnight in Coolah on the "Black Stump Way".
*(Approx. 450Kms)*

**Day 2;**  From Coolah, heading toward Coonabarabran for brunch. The section between Binnaway and Coonabarabran is well acknowledged for bikes. \
From Coonabarabran, a scenic route through the Warrumbungles to Gulargambone, then across to Nyngan and the Big Bogan. After sinking a tinnie in a singlet, we'll head south west to the Geographical Centre of NSW, finishing the day at the Rabbit Trap Hotel in Albert.
*(Approx. 475Kms)*

**Day 3;**  From Albert, we head to Parkes (or Trundle) for brunch then backroads to Conowindra and Woodstock for a final run south via Wyangala Dam, Rugby and Dalton, before finishing back at Gunning.
*(Approx. 420Kms)*
